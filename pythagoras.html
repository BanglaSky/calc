What is Pythagoras' theorem

Pythagoras' Theorem is the idea that the square on the hypotenuse of a right-angled triangle is equal in area to the sum of the squares on the other two sides.

What is a hypotenuse?

The hypotenuse is the longest side of a right-angled triangle.

How to find the hypotenuse

You can compare all the sides of the triangle to find the longest side

Or you can use the formula "a² + b² = c²"and then square root the whole thing
